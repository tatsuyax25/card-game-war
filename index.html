How to Play War

War is a simple card game for two players.

  • Each player gets half the deck (26 cards)
  • Click "Flip Cards" to reveal the top card from each deck
  • Higher card wins both cards (Ace is highest)
  • If cards tie, it's WAR! Each player plays 3 face-down cards, then 1 face-up. Highest wins all cards
  • Game ends when one player has all 52 cards
